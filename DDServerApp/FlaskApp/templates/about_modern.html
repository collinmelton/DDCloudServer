About EXACT

General Information

EXACT (EXpert Annotation of Clinical Trials) is a web application that is designed as a crowd sourcing platform for annotation of cancer clinical trials from ClinicalTrials.gov. The application provides an interface for users to annotate Cancer Type, Drugs, and Molecular Information in each clinical trial. Molecular information could be used in the trial either as enrollment criteria or studied as part of the trial. The intent of this website is to provide detailed annotations in order to enable better tools to both research the current state of personalized medicine in cancer as well as to potentially enable phsyician and patient guided searches for clinical trials based on molecular phenotypes.

If you are interested in participating please sign up and start annotating!

Ontologies

For diseases we are using the Human Disease Ontology .

For drugs we are using drug names from DrugBank with additional drug synonyms from PubChem .

For genes we are using gene names from HGNC .
